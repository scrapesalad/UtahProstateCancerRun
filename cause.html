Our Mission

Utah Prostate Cancer Run & Walk is dedicated to ending prostate cancer by advancing research, increasing awareness, and providing support to patients and their families. Through events like this Run/Walk, we bring together communities to make a tangible difference in the fight against this disease.

Prostate cancer is the second leading cause of cancer death in American men, but together we can change that statistic. Your participation and fundraising efforts directly contribute to groundbreaking research, patient support programs, and life-saving early detection initiatives.

Running Towards a Future Without Prostate Cancer

Every step we take together brings us closer to our goal of zero prostate cancer deaths and improved quality of life for patients and survivors.

How Your Support Makes an Impact

Research Advancements

Your fundraising helps fund cutting-edge research into early detection methods, personalized treatments, and ultimately a cure for prostate cancer.

Patient Support

We provide resources, support groups, and educational materials to help patients and families navigate their prostate cancer journey.

Community Awareness

Events like this Run/Walk spread awareness about risk factors, screening options, and the importance of early detection.

Join Us in the Fight Against Prostate Cancer

Whether you run, walk, donate, or volunteer, you're helping save lives and create a future free from prostate cancer.
