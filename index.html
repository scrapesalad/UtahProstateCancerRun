What is Prostate Cancer?

Prostate cancer is the most common cancer among men (after skin cancer). It develops in the prostate, a small gland that produces seminal fluid.

Early Detection Saves Lives

Early-stage prostate cancer often has no symptoms. Regular screening (PSA test, digital rectal exam) is key for early detection and better outcomes.

Know the Risk Factors

Risk increases with age, family history, and being African American. Talk to your doctor about your risk and screening options.

About The Event

The Utah Prostate Cancer Run is a celebration of life, health, and community coming together to support prostate cancer awareness and research.

Join hundreds of participants in beautiful Salt Lake Valley for this inspirational event featuring a 5K run/walk and 1 mile family walk through Wheeler Historic Farm.

All proceeds benefit the Utah Prostate Cancer Foundation and local support services.

SUMMIT UROLOGY PROSTATE CANCER FOUNDATION

Our mission is to reduce the burden of prostate cancer on patients and their families by direct assistance to patients as well as by funding programs designed to provide early detection, prevention and treatment options in the State of Utah.

Our goals include the following:

  • Contribute direct financial assistance for local prostate cancer patients in need
  • Donate annually to national and/or local foundations that conduct prostate cancer research in the area of prevention, detection and treatment.
  • Support and sponsor events to educate men and families on prostate cancer detection through community educational programs
  • Help fund or sponsor local cancer survivor programs and support groups
  • Sponsor the Utah Prostate Cancer Run and Walk 5k annually in September (prostate cancer awareness month) as a fundraiser and vehicle to raise awareness in the community.
