About the Event

Running with Purpose

The Utah Prostate Cancer Run is more than just a marathon - it's a movement. Since 2014, we've brought together runners from across the country to raise awareness and funds for prostate cancer research and patient support.

This year, we're proud to partner with the Utah Cancer Foundation to direct 100% of race proceeds to local prostate cancer initiatives, including free screenings for underserved communities and cutting-edge research at the Huntsman Cancer Institute.

Our Mission

To eliminate prostate cancer through awareness, early detection, and research funding while building a supportive community for patients and families.

Understanding Prostate Cancer

Knowledge is Power

Early Detection

Prostate cancer is the second most commonly diagnosed cancer among U.S. men. 1 in 9 men will be diagnosed with prostate cancer in their lifetime. Early detection through regular screenings is crucial for successful treatment.

Support Resources

Connect with advocacy groups that provide education and support:

  • ZERO—The End of Prostate Cancer
  • Prostate Cancer Foundation
  • Prostate Health Education Network
  • Us TOO Support Network

Living with Prostate Cancer

Managing your health while living with prostate cancer includes:

  • Regular exercise and physical activity
  • Proper nutrition and diet
  • Mental health support
  • Regular medical check-ups

Support cutting-edge research and clinical trials that are advancing prostate cancer treatment.
