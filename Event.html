Parking Information

Free parking is available at Wheeler Historic Farm. Additional parking is available at the adjacent park with overflow parking at Murray High School (5440 S State St). Shuttles will run continuously from the high school to the event site from 7:00 AM to 11:30 AM.

Please arrive early to allow time for parking and getting to the starting line.

Event Activities

The Utah Prostate Cancer Run & Walk is more than just a race - it's a celebration of life, health, and community. In addition to the run/walk events, participants can enjoy:

  • Health & wellness expo with local vendors
  • Free prostate cancer screenings (provided by Summit Urology Group)
  • Kids activities and face painting
  • Live music and entertainment
  • Survivor recognition ceremony
  • Post-race refreshments

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I register on event day?
Yes! Day-of registration will be available from 7:30 AM to 8:45 AM. However, we encourage you to register online in advance to guarantee your t-shirt size and avoid lines.
Are strollers allowed?
Yes, strollers are welcome on both the 5K and 1 Mile courses. For safety reasons, we ask that strollers start near the back of the pack.
Can I bring my dog?
While we love pets, for the safety and comfort of all participants, we ask that you leave your furry friends at home for this event.
What if it rains?
The event will take place rain or shine. In case of severe weather, announcements will be made on our website and social media channels.
Is there a bag check?
Yes, a secure bag check will be available near the registration area. Please do not leave valuables in checked bags.
